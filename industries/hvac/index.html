Common questions

What HVAC owners ask us.

Will it work with ServiceTitan or Housecall Pro? +

Yes. We build on top of the dispatch and CRM software you already run — ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, or whatever you use. The goal is to make your existing stack smarter, not replace it.

Can the call catcher tell a real emergency from a routine call? +

Yes. We train it on your definition of an emergency — no heat in winter, no cooling in a heat wave, a gas smell. True emergencies get routed to your on-call tech immediately; everything else is booked for the next available window.

How fast can this be running before summer? +

Most first projects go live in one to two weeks. If you call us in spring, the after-hours catcher will be running well before your peak season hits.
